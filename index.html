Abby Sigler is a freelance journalist and Managing Editor of The Guardsman.

Based in San Francisco, she reports on community, culture, and technology, with an interest in how these forces intersect in everyday life. Her investigative reporting has received awards from the San Francisco Press Club and the NorCal Society of Professional Journalists.

Abby graduated from UC San Diego with a degree in Cognitive Science and has completed a certificate in Data Journalism at City College. At The Guardsman, she has covered education policy, student life, and San Francisco's shifting cultural landscape. She approaches journalism as both documentation and critique—an effort to reflect life in the city while examining the structures that shape it.
